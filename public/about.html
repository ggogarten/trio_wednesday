About PoliPoints API

AlchemyAPI is capable of automatically tagging your HTML, text, or web-based content.
We employ sophisticated natural language processing technology to analyze your data,
tagging your information in a manner similar to human-based tagging.
AlchemyAPI's concept tagging API is capable of abstraction, understanding how concepts
relate and tagging accordingly ("My favorite brands are BMW, Ferrari, and Porsche." = "Automotive Industry").
